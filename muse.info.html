Muse is a website created by four teenagers to help others with their learning ability in music. We have gathered as much information as we could to help you out on your journey in the world of music. Our website helps you to learn about the key signatures, time signatures, the treble clef, bass clef, and of courseabout us. We all have some form of interest in music and thought that it would be cool to help others out in learning it as well.
